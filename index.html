Core Research Areas

Laser Diagnostics

Development and application of advanced laser techniques for non-intrusive measurements in complex energy systems, focusing on combustion, engines, fuel cells, and photovoltaics.

  • In-situ monitoring of spray and combustion processes.
  • Efficiency mapping of fuel cell membranes.

Ultrafast Science

Investigating fundamental light–matter interaction dynamics and ultra-short transient phenomena using femtosecond and picosecond laser systems.

  • Probing carrier dynamics in novel semiconductor materials.
  • High-speed, time-resolved imaging of complex flows.

Space Science & Optics

Research and development in advanced optical systems, including adaptive optics for space telescopes, and thermal & hyperspectral imaging for Earth observation and planetary science.

  • Hardware-in-the-loop testing of adaptive optics.
  • Data processing for hyperspectral remote sensing.

Biomedical Imaging & AI

Developing new **lab-on-chip diagnostics** by combining high-resolution optical microscopy with deep learning algorithms for automated healthcare analysis.

  • AI-assisted cell classification and disease screening.
  • Microfluidic platform design for point-of-care testing.

Ongoing Projects

Sustainable Tech

Next-Gen Optical Technologies for Energy

Developing cost-effective, high-throughput optical systems to analyze and optimize sustainable energy devices, specifically advanced solar cells and hydrogen fuel production methods.

Ultrafast Imaging

Femtosecond Spectroscopy of Light-Matter Dynamics

A project dedicated to capturing dynamic changes in materials at the picosecond and femtosecond scales using pump-probe and structured illumination techniques.

AI & Healthcare

AI-Powered Microfluidic Diagnostics

Integrating deep convolutional neural networks with custom-built microfluidic chips for rapid, automated, and accurate detection of diseases at the point of care.
