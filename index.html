Advanced Imaging & Sensing Lab (AISL)

Innovation in Optics, Imaging, and AI-Powered Diagnostics

Welcome to Advanced Imaging & Sensing Lab (AISL)

We are a dynamic research group at IIT Jodhpur dedicated to pushing the boundaries of optical science and engineering. Our interdisciplinary work combines fundamental ultrafast science with applied innovations in biomedical imaging, sustainable energy diagnostics, and space technology.

Core Research Areas

Laser Diagnostics

Advanced non-intrusive measurements for energy systems, combustion, and fuel cells.

Ultrafast Science

Investigating light-matter interactions on femtosecond and picosecond timescales.

Space Science & Optics

Developing adaptive optics and hyperspectral imaging for space applications.

Biomedical Imaging & AI

Creating lab-on-chip diagnostics using microscopy and deep learning.
